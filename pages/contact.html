IRC

Our official #uyuni channel is hosted at Freenode. Join with your favourite IRC client, or use the web application.

Currently we do not require a registered nickname to join, but it is required to talk talk because of recent spambot attacks to Freenode.

Mailing lists

  • uyuni-announce: To receive news about the Uyuni (moderated).
  • uyuni-devel: To discuss about Uyuni development (anyone can post as long as it is registered).
  • uyuni-users: To discuss about Uyuni usage (anyone can post as long as it is registered).

Reporting bugs

Submit bugs to the issues tab located at our Github repository.

Verify that a duplicate of the issue does not already exist!
